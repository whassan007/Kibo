# SKILL: Advisory, Training & Incident Response — United States

## Purpose
Provide Privacy-by-Design advisory, deliver targeted training, and execute breach response under 50-state notification law plus HIPAA and FTC health-breach rules.

## Controlling frameworks & key obligations
- **Breach notification:** all 50 states + DC require notice to affected residents (timing/triggers vary; some require AG notice over a threshold count).
- **Sectoral overlays:** HIPAA Breach Notification Rule (≤60 days; HHS + individuals + media for large breaches); FTC Health Breach Notification Rule for non-HIPAA health apps.
- **CCPA private right of action:** for breaches of defined personal information due to failure to maintain reasonable security — statutory damages exposure.
- **Training:** role-specific curricula; remedial micro-training on policy deviation.

## Operational workflow
1. On suspected compromise, trigger containment (revoke credentials, isolate systems) and preserve forensic evidence.
2. Identify data elements and states of residence affected; build the multi-state notification matrix.
3. Determine the **shortest** applicable deadline across all jurisdictions and overlays.
4. Draft individual + regulator notifications; prepare substitute notice if volume/contact gaps require it.
5. Auto-assign remedial training to implicated employees.
6. Route final notifications and timing to human counsel for authorization.

## Statutory deadlines & thresholds
- HIPAA: notify without unreasonable delay and no later than **60 days**.
- State deadlines range from "without unreasonable delay" to fixed windows (e.g., 30–45 days in several states).
- Notify to the **strictest** applicable clock.

## Guardrails (mandatory)
- Never suppress or delay a required notification for reputational reasons.
- Preserve evidence before remediation where feasible.
- Escalate timing/notifiability judgment to human counsel.
